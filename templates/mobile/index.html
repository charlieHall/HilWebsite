About Me

Hi, my name is Hilary and I am a fully qualified therapist working in Brighton and Hove. I offer a warm and supportive space to help people explore the difficulties they are facing in life.

I have a lovely counselling room that is very private in a tranquil setting, where you will be heard and understood as we make sense of how you are feeling and work to find a way forward that is right for you.

Making that first step to start counselling can feel very daunting and so I am happy to respond by email or phone and try to answer any questions you may have. I work with individuals over the age of 18 years and have been able to help the people I work with make a real difference to their lives. I would be delighted to help you do the same.
